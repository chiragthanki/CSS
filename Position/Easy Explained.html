CSS Positions

Box-1-Static
-Top, right, Bottom, left values don't work with static position.
Box-2-Relative
- Position is relative to "box-1 static".
- Counts from its above element's position
Box-3-Absolute
-COUNT'S FROM TOP-LEFT CORNER irrespective of its parent.
-Here, it is in last div blank-space but it ignores it and counts its position from viewport only.
Box-4-Sticky
-Sticks to its parent. - i.e. div.sticky-container.
-Unsticks when its parent ends
Box-5-Fixed
-Works with only top, right, bottom, left values.
-Counts from viewport.
-Sticks to the screen irrespective of scrolling.
-Once fixed doesn't change

Q.1 Why border color is "white" in box-1 when given only to font?

Ans. Because if we have given border property without specifying color like this: "Border:"2px solid" and div/container/font color is given, then border will automatically take its parent/font/container color.

E.g.

  • border: 2px solid
  • color:white
  • then this will take "white" color as a "border-color"
